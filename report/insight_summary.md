# Insight Summary

This SQL analysis examined Nigerian music streaming performance using key music KPIs such as total streams, save rate, skip rate, completion rate, and playlist conversion.

## Key Findings

City Boys II by Burna Boy recorded the highest total streams, showing that it currently has the largest audience in the dataset.

Rush Again by Ayra Starr had the highest save rate and was the only song that met the investment-ready criteria. The criteria included a save rate above 20%, skip rate below 10%, and completion rate above 80%.

Afropop had the highest average save rate, mainly because of the strong performance of Rush Again by Ayra Starr.

## Recommendation

Based on the analysis, Rush Again by Ayra Starr should receive additional promotional support. Although City Boys II has the highest total streams, Rush Again shows stronger audience engagement, lower listener drop-off, and better long-term growth potential.

Recommended actions include playlist pitching, TikTok promotion, and targeted social media campaigns.
